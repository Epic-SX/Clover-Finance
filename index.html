CLOVER

    An Open DeFi Platform Powered by Polkadot

    Clover network is a connector between decentralized assets and centralized exchanges. Powered by Polkadot parachain technology, Clover provides a platform for DeFi applications and cross-chain asset interactions.

    Whitepaper

    Clover Services

    Swap
    Trade assets directly from user’s wallet. Platform supports all kinds of trading pairs
    Wrapper
    Trustlessly exchange assets between chains with no centralized parties involved
    Vault
    Receive tradeable staking reward, unlocking the derivative value of staked assets
    Lending
    Deposit assets to earn interest or borrow
    Stablecoins
    Support native stablecoin issuance function to fulfill decentralized assets without single point of failure
    Governance
    Being a parachain on Polkadot’s network of interoperable specialized blockchains allows Clover to scale seamlessly

    Clover Services

    Scalable
    Clover can scale seamlessly as a parachain of Polkadot. It enables scalability by al-lowing specialized blockchains to communicate with each other in a secure and trustless environment. Clover can communicate with other parachain projects through the Polkadot platform
    Cross-chain
    Allows interoperability with other parachain assets through cross-chain message passing enabled by Polkadot technology. Cross-chain transaction are available and assets can be transferred on different
    Sustainable
    Instant settlement and low transaction fee. No increasing gas fees and congestions of the network. Friendly to retail users
    Trustless
    A completely non-custodial P2P trading platform where only account holders can access their own funds. no centralized governance entity. Token holders have control over the system
    Private
    Send transactions anonymously without providing any personal information
    Open Platform
    Developers are encouraged to contribute to modules and applications. Community can propose, vote, and implement changes through decentralized governance module
