Next-generation services for the next of wave of DeFi users

Bithumb.io is a DeFi ecosystem offering fully decentralized on-chain trading, a decentralized money market for lending and borrowing as well as a staking liquidity protocol. Built as a parachain on Polkadot, it is fast, cheap and secure and allows for cross-chain swaps. Bithumb.io is run by the community via on-chain governance and distributes profits to BXB token holders.

Intuitive
Trades are settled automatically at a price determined by the liquidity of the two assets of a pair via an automated market maker (AMM).
Provide liquidity
The default fee is set to 0.3% and will be proportionally distributed to liquidity providers.
Open
Any trading pair can be supported after a governance vote.
Earn interest
Deposit assets to lend them out to others and receive interest in return.
Borrow funds
Provide collateral (including staked assets via our staking liquidity protocol) to receive a loan in supported tokens. Allows you to put your assets to work, leverage your longs or build a short position on an asset.
Algorithmically controlled
The interest rate is set dynamically depending on supply and demand dynamics.
Liquid
The protocol tokenizes staked assets to make them tradeable. Stake tokens (e.g. DOT) in a pool and receive pool tokens (e.g. SDOT) representing your share of the total amount in the pool.
Unlocking the value of staked assets
Use your pool tokens in other protocols across all chains on the Polkadot network. For instance, use them as collateral to borrow assets on our money market protocol.
Algorithmically secured
The collateralization rate is determined by an algorithm to increase capital efficiency without sacrificing security.
